History

The original house was built in the 1900s. This picture is of Arthur and Addie Davis with their dog Tupper and horse Harvey

In 2008, a fire destroyed the Heart of the Valley gift shop and most of its contents. After year, a new gift shop structure was built. Now bigger than ever, the gift shop is open all year round.
